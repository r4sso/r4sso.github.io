### About this website
This site is a personal space where I share my knowledge, experiences, and stories. You'll find articles on various topics, including the research I conduct and notes about things I want to remember.

- Share my knowledge that might help others.

This site is built with [Hugo](https://gohugo.io/) & [Tailwindss](https://tailwindcss.com/), meant to be simple and lightweight.

#### History of this site 
In 2020, I created this site for fun using plain HTML + CSS.

2021 and 2022, I stumbled upon the static site generator `jekyll`. After some exploring and tinkering with jekyll, I even experimented with different free domain names like `r4sso.cf`.

In Early 2023, after trying out numerous themes without finding the perfect fit, I create my own personalized theme using `bootstrap`. Around that time, I also migrated my site to `hugo` and `tailwindcss`.
